Fiber
Material
Plant fibers used for crafting darts, fire-starting tools, and some legend items.
Biome: Dense jungle, near saplings
Coords: Common around ~46W / 25S and other forested areas

Comes from chopping small saplings and thin young plants. Look for thin, straight saplings and cut them with any bladed tool; fiber often drops with small sticks.

A small bundle of thin, brownish strands or shavings, often in loose piles on the ground after chopping.

  • Darts and blowgun darts
  • Hand drill / fire bow components
  • Certain Spirits of Amazonia legend/ritual crafts
Rope (Liana)
Material
Core binding material used in almost all tools, weapons, armor, and many structures.
Biome: Jungle trees with hanging vines
Coords: Very common; e.g. 46W / 26S, 50W / 27S

Harvest from green vines wrapped around tree trunks at about head height. Approach the vine and use the harvest interaction to collect rope.

Thick green vine spiraling around tree trunks, often multiple per tree.

  • Stone axe, stone spear, bows, armor pieces
  • Fishing rod, traps, shelters and frames
  • Most mid‚Äë and late‚Äëgame constructions
