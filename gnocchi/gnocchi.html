⚠️ LARGE BATCH: Split dough into multiple mixes.

1. The Base (Gnocchi)

Ingredients

AMB GUST TECH: Bake potatoes whole on a bed of salt in the pizza oven (residual heat ~200°C). Do NOT boil. Baking = dry potato = crispy fry.
1. Bake potatoes until tender (45-60m).
2. Peel & riced while HOT (Crucial).
3. Mix ingredients. Do not overwork.
4. Roll to 2cm thick slab. Cut 2x2cm squares.
5. Blanch in boiling water until they float.
6. Shock in ice water. Oil & Store.

2. Salsa Chistorra-Sambal

Ingredients

FLAVOR HACK: Render chistorra starting from a COLD pan to get maximum fat extraction before adding onions.
1. Render chistorra meat (skin off).
2. Add onion/garlic -> Sofrito.
3. Deglaze w/ Vinegar.
4. Add Tomato, Sambal, Spices.
5. Simmer 20m. Blitz smooth.
