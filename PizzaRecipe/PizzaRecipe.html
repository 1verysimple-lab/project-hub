Protocol: Pizza

Logic: 1 Pizza = 250g Dough Ball.
Recommendation: 1 per Adult, 0.5–1 per Child. Always round up for leftovers.
Target: 65% Hydration Dough
  • Strong Flour 0 g
  • Water (Cold) 0 ml
  • Salt 0 g
  • Fresh Yeast 0 g
*Use 1/3 weight if using Dry Instant Yeast.
Target: Raw Neapolitan Sauce
  • San Marzano Tomatoes 0 g
  • Sea Salt 0 g
  • Fresh Basil Leaves 0
  • Olive Oil Splash
*Approx. 80-90g sauce per pizza.

Execution

Mix & Autolyse: Dissolve yeast in water. Mix with flour until combined. Let sit for 30 mins.
Knead: Add salt. Knead 10 mins until smooth and tacky.
Bulk Ferment: Cover airtight. Room temp for 18–24 hours.
The Balling: Divide into 250g portions. Shape into tight balls. Rest 2–4 hours.
The Sauce: Hand-crush tomatoes (remove hard stems). Stir in salt and basil. Do not cook.
The Bake: Stretch dough. Apply sauce. Top. Bake at max heat (Steel: 2-3 mins / Stone: 5-7 mins).
OPTIMIZATION NOTES

Topping Strategy: Less is more. Heavy toppings prevent the rise. Use low-moisture mozzarella (Fior di Latte) to avoid "soup pizza".

The "Leopard" Effect: If your home oven lacks heat, use the "Frying Pan Hack": Cook base in a scorching dry frying pan for 2 mins (to crisp bottom), then transfer to the very top shelf under the grill/broiler for 1-2 mins (to char crust).

Variation (The White Pie): Ditch the tomato. Use a base of ricotta, garlic oil, thyme, and lemon zest. Finished with pistachio.
