Gold Standard Waffles

Multiplier: 1x Yields: ~7 Waffles

Mise en Place (Total Shopping List)

Process Breakdown

1. Dry Mix
    2. Wet Mix
      3. The Finish (Post-Rise)

        Execution

        01 Dry Mix: Whisk flour, sugar, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.
        02 Wet Mix: Gradually whisk in warm milk (40°C), melted butter, and vanilla until smooth.
        03 The Rise: Cover bowl. Let sit at room temp.
        Tip: For sourdough-like depth, fridge overnight after this hour.
        60:00
        04 Final Mix: Whisk in eggs and baking soda just before cooking. Batter will be thin.
        05 Cook: Preheat iron. Pour batter (don't overfill). Cook until steam stops rising.
