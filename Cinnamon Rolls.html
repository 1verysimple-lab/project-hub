    2. Dough
      3. Filling
        4. Glaze (Standard Icing)

          Execution

          01 Tangzhong: Whisk ingredients over medium heat until thick paste. Cool to room temp.
          02 Dough: Mix Tangzhong, cold milk, egg, butter, sugar, yeast. Add flour & salt. Knead 12 mins.
          03 Bulk Proof: 90 Mins.
          Note: Faster at 35°C, Slower/Better if cooled.
          90:00
          04 Shape: Roll 30x45cm. Spread butter, sugar/cinn mix. Roll tight. Cut with dental floss.
          05 Second Proof: 45-60 Mins until touching.
          45:00
          06 Bake: 175°C (350°F). Aim for 88°C internal temp.
          20:00
          07 The Glace Icing: While rolls bake, sift the icing sugar. Whisk in water slowly until you get a thick, pourable consistency. Drizzle over rolls when they are warm (not piping hot).
