The Protocol: Chicken

01. The Kernel (Base Stock)

DEPENDENCY

Input

  • Chicken Legs, Wings, Carcass
  • Onion 2, halved
  • Carrots 2, rough chop
  • Celery 2 stalks
  • Garlic 1 head, halved
  • Aromatics Bay, Thyme, Peppercorns

Execution

  1. The Maillard: Brown the bones heavily in the pot. Do not crowd. We want fond, not steam.
  2. Deglaze: Add vegetables. Cover with cold water (approx 3L). Scrape the bottom.
  3. Simmer: Bring to a boil, then immediately reduce to the barest simmer.
  4. Maintain: Cook for 2.5 - 3 hours. Skim impurities occasionally.
  5. Strain: Pass through a fine sieve. Discard solids. Pick the meat off the legs for later.

02. Variations (The Endpoints)

CHOOSE ONE

V1. The Penicillin (Classic)

Restorative. Unpretentious. Heavy pepper.

  • + Diced Carrot & Celery
  • + Egg Noodles
  • + Fresh Dill/Parsley

Simmer base with veg (10m). Add noodles until al dente. Finish with shredded leg meat and aggressive black pepper.

V2. Velouté Light (Mushroom)

Rich but not heavy. Cold weather spec.

  • + Browned Mushrooms
  • + Cream / Milk
  • + Fresh Tarragon

Sauté mushrooms separately. Add to broth. Temper in cream (do not boil). Finish with tarragon.

V3. The Sitges Detox

Clean, sharp, medicinal.

  • + Ginger & Chili (Infuse)
  • + Soy Sauce
  • + Lime Juice

Infuse broth with ginger/chili (15m). Season with soy. Kill heat, add lime juice immediately before serving.

V4. Rustico (Bean & Kale)

A meal, not a starter. Hearty.

  • + White Beans
  • + Chopped Kale
  • + Parmesan Rind

Simmer rind in broth. Add beans and greens. Cook until tender. Serve with olive oil drizzle.

V5. The Golden Healer

Anti-inflammatory. Bright. Fragrant.

  • + Turmeric (1 tsp)
  • + Basmati Rice
  • + Lemon

Bloom turmeric in oil in the pot first. Add broth and rice. Simmer 15 mins. Finish with lemon.

B. Lazy Milanese

The Chef's cheat.

  • Press into breadcrumbs.
  • Shallow fry until golden.

C. Soy & Honey Glaze

Sticky & savoury.

  • Score meat. Salt/Pepper.
