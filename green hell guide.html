Survival Logic: The 3 Death Traps

THE DIRT FACTOR

Eating with muddy hands gives Parasites. Parasites drain your nutrition 2x faster. Always wash in a river before eating.

SANITY SPIRAL

Leeches, insomnia, and raw food lower sanity. At 0 sanity, you will hallucinate attackers. Stand near a fire or sleep to recover.

VENOM PROTOCOL

Snake bites kill in minutes. Do not just bandage. You MUST use a Tobacco Bandage or eat Plantain Lily to stop the poison.

Navigation Logic for Co-op

  • Sync Coordinates: Check watch (F) every 30 seconds while moving to ensure you aren't drifting apart.
  • The "Follower" Rule: One player leads with the map/compass, the other watches the environment for snakes.
  • Building Trails: Drop single Small Stones or Sticks at path forks to find your way home.
