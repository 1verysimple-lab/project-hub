Water Strategy

  • • Find Coconuts. Drink milk, then use bowl to collect rain.
  • • Use Turtle Shells as massive water collectors.
  • • Never drink "Dirty" water unless you have a Charcoal or Bone Soup.

Co-op Strategy

  • • Watch Each Other: One player cuts trees, the other watches for predators.
  • • The Medic: Keep Molineria leaves in your pack for instant bandages.
  • • Share XP: Blueprints found by one are shared by both.
