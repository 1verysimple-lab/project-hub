Tactical Briefing

1. Spatial Sound (The Concept)

Your brain is a radar. By processing tiny delays and volume changes between your left and right ears, you can pinpoint exactly where a sound is coming from in 3D space. This app simulates that process.

2. Mission Rules

  • - Listen for the sniper shot.
  • - Tap the grid tile where the sound originated.
  • - 1st Hit (< 0.5s): 15 Pts + 10 Dmg (Perfect).
  • - 1st Hit (> 0.5s): 10 Pts + 10 Dmg.
  • - 2nd/3rd Hit: Reduced points & damage.
  • - If you MISS, you take damage and he stays put.
  • - If you HIT, he relocates.

3. The Science (HRTF)

How do stereo headphones fake "Rear" audio?

The game uses a Head-Related Transfer Function. It applies specific filters to the sound wave to mimic how your outer ear (pinna) muffles sounds coming from behind you. This spectral filtering tricks your brain into perceiving depth on a 2D headset.
